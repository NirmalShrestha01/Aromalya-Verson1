ABOUT US

We were born and raised in the Himalayas. Growing up, we enjoyed nature’s offering while protecting the land, fauna and flora. We had no doctors, medical facilities or fancy shops for everyday essentials, so we totally relied on natural remedies and natural solutions for living. We started Aromalaya to bring the same finest and rarest of herbs and essential oils from the Himalayas. While sourcing our herbs and oils, we take extreme caution on ensuring sustainable harvest, and utilization of local and indifenous people for collection and processing.
